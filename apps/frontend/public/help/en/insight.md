# Analytics & Insights Feature Guide

## Overview

The Analytics & Insights feature helps you understand how your Chatbot is performing through data such as query volume, Q&A performance, and user feedback. Use these insights to optimize your knowledge base and improve answer quality.

## Time Range

The time range selector at the top lets you switch between different reporting periods:

- **7 days**: View data from the past week
- **30 days**: View data from the past month (default)
- **90 days**: View data from the past three months

Click the "Refresh" button to reload the latest data.

## Key Metrics (KPI Cards)

### Total Queries

Shows the total number of queries in the selected time range, with growth rate compared to the previous period (↑ or ↓).

### Total FAQs

Shows the total number of Q&A cards in your Chatbot, including how many are in active status.

### Query Success Rate

Shows the percentage of queries that found a matching answer. A higher success rate indicates better knowledge base coverage.

### Feedback Status

Shows the percentage of queries where users provided feedback (helpful / not helpful / viewed).

## Chart Sections

### User Feedback Distribution

A bar chart showing the distribution of three feedback types:

- **👍 Helpful**: Users found the answer helpful
- **👎 Not helpful**: Users did not find the answer helpful
- **👁 Viewed**: Users viewed the result but did not provide feedback

### Topic Distribution

Shows how queries are distributed across categories (Topics), helping you understand which subjects users ask about most.

## Zero Result Queries

This section shows the number of queries that did not find any matching answer within the selected time range.

**How to use:**

- Click the section header to expand or collapse the detailed list
- When expanded, you can view the actual query content
- For zero-result queries, click "Add Q&A" to create a new Q&A card from that query, filling gaps in your knowledge base

## Top FAQs (Top 10)

Shows the 10 most frequently matched Q&A cards.

**How to use:**

- Click the section header to expand or collapse the list
- When expanded, view the ranking, question content, and hit count
- Hover over an item and click the "Edit" button to edit that Q&A directly

## Top Disliked FAQs (Top 10)

Shows the 10 Q&A cards that received the most "not helpful" feedback. These are priority candidates for optimization.

**How to use:**

- Click the section header to expand or collapse the list
- When expanded, view the ranking, question content, and dislike count
- Hover over an item and click the "Edit" button to improve the answer content

## Best Practices

1. **Review zero-result queries regularly**: Add common zero-result queries as new Q&A cards to quickly improve answer coverage
2. **Optimize disliked answers**: For Q&A with high negative feedback, refine the answer content or add synonyms
3. **Monitor topic distribution**: If a topic has high query volume but few Q&A cards, consider adding more content to that category
4. **Track query success rate**: Aim to maintain or improve the success rate to ensure your knowledge base continues to meet user needs
